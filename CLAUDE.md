# AI Integration Full-Day Training Workshop

A full-day (~8 hour) training workshop produced by the Translational AI Center (TrAC) at Iowa State University. Prepares professionals to lead AI Integration within their organizations.

---

## Audience

Mid-level managers, business leaders, and non-technical professionals who need to lead or influence AI adoption. Industry-agnostic by default, with examples from manufacturing, agriculture, design, and education. ISU / TrAC branded throughout.

---
